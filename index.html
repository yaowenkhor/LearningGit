About NewJeans

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, a subsidiary of HYBE Corporation. The group consists of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein. They debuted on July 22, 2022, with the release of their attention-grabbing single "Attention."

Known for their nostalgic Y2K aesthetic and refreshing sound that combines elements of R&B, pop, hip-hop, and dance music, NewJeans quickly rose to prominence in the K-pop industry. Their name has a dual meaning - referring to both timeless jeans that never go out of style and the idea of creating new genes for the music industry.
